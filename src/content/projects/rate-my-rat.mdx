## Context & Background
HouseHouse made canvas dog houses and had a batch of small versions that were marketed as "rat houses". To my surprise, there were communities of rate owners that were very proud of their pets. I wanted to build something that would get the community engaged so we could market the rat houses to them.

## Implementation
I build a website with Drupal where rat lovers could register and upload a picture of their rats. Other users could swipe through a list of community generated images and rate them. I also added a leaderboard to encourage competition. I then build a single-page website for the rat houses with a purchase button and advertised the website in the rat communities.

## What Went Well
Rate my rat was fairly popular and had a lot of traffic. The landing page for the rat houses had it's first purchase within 20 minutes of the first advertising post.
